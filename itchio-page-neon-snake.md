## Long Description
**The snake you know. The neon glow you didn't expect. 🐍🌈**

Neon Snake takes the timeless snake formula and wraps it in dazzling rainbow neon aesthetics. Swipe to turn, eat to grow, don't bite yourself. Sounds familiar — but the look, feel, and polish make this the definitive browser snake experience.

**🌈 RAINBOW NEON AESTHETICS**
Every move your snake makes leaves a vibrant neon trail that shifts through the rainbow spectrum. The longer your snake, the more spectacular the color show. It looks absolutely stunning at high scores.

**📱 SWIPE CONTROLS**
Built for touch-first gameplay. Swipe in any direction to turn. Keyboard arrow keys also work perfectly for desktop players. The controls feel tight and responsive — no accidental turns.

**♾️ ENDLESS MODE**
The classic: survive as long as possible. Eat food to grow, dodge your own body, keep going until you fill the entire screen (or run out of room). No time limits — just pure survival.

**🏆 HIGH SCORE CHASE**
Your best score is saved locally. Chase your personal best, then chase it again. Posting a high score screenshot to your friends is basically mandatory.

**⚡ SPEED BOOST OPTION**
Feeling brave? Enable speed boost mode for a faster snake that requires sharper reflexes. Scores are multiplied — high risk, high reward.

**WHY YOU'LL LOVE IT**
- The most visually satisfying snake game in a browser
- Silky smooth controls on both touch and keyboard
- Instantly recognizable gameplay — anyone can play
- No account needed, plays offline
- Pay what you want — totally free to enjoy

**Suggested minimum: $1** — but enjoy it for free if you want. 🐍
