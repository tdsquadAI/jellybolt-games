## Long Description
**Conquer the grid. Dominate the board. Outsmart the AI. ⚔️**

Code Conquest is a turn-based strategy game where you battle a cunning AI opponent for control of an 8×8 grid. Place your pieces, capture territory, and execute clever tactics to secure victory — every move matters.

**🎯 STRATEGIC TERRITORY CONTROL**
Each turn you and the AI claim squares on the board. Surround enemy territory to capture it. Think two steps ahead — aggressive expansion leaves you vulnerable, but playing too safe lets the AI dominate.

**🤖 SMART AI OPPONENT**
The AI adapts to your playstyle. It's not just random — it actively tries to cut off your expansion and protect its territory. A genuine challenge whether you're a strategy veteran or a newcomer.

**⚡ FAST-PACED ROUNDS**
Games last 5–10 minutes. Perfect for a quick session or a best-of-three showdown with yourself (or your friends watching over your shoulder).

**🏆 MULTIPLE DIFFICULTY TIERS**
Start with normal AI and work up to hard mode where the opponent is ruthless. Can you crack the perfect strategy?
