# Privacy Policy — JellyBolt Games

**Effective Date:** March 1, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 1, 2026

## Introduction

JellyBolt Games ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile applications ("Apps"), including **BrainRot Quiz Battle**, **Code Conquest**, and any other games published under the JellyBolt Games brand.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal data.**

Our Apps are designed to be used without creating an account, signing in, or providing any personal information. Specifically:

- **No account registration required** — You can play our games immediately after downloading.
- **No personal information collected** — We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.
- **No location data** — We do not access or track your location.
- **No contacts or photos** — We do not access your contacts, camera, or photo library.
- **No usage analytics in MVP** — Our initial releases do not include analytics or tracking tools.

## Game Data

All game data (scores, progress, preferences) is stored **locally on your device only**. We do not transmit game data to any server. If you uninstall the App, this local data will be deleted.

## Advertising

Our MVP (Minimum Viable Product) releases **do not contain any advertisements**. If we add advertising in future updates, we will:

1. Update this Privacy Policy before ads are introduced.
2. Clearly disclose what ad networks are used.
3. Provide information about any data the ad networks may collect.
4. Comply with all applicable advertising regulations.

## Third-Party Services

Our Apps do not currently use any third-party services that collect data. If we integrate third-party services in the future (such as analytics, crash reporting, or ad networks), we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly.

## Children's Privacy

Our Apps are rated **Everyone** and are safe for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal data, our Apps comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

## Data Sharing

**We do not share any data with third parties** because we do not collect any data in the first place.

## Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal data on our servers, there is no personal data at risk. All game data remains on your device.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. If you have any questions about your data, feel free to contact us.
