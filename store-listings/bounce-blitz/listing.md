## Full Description

Think you've got reflexes? ⚡💥 Prove it in Bounce Blitz — the hyper-casual one-tap arcade game that's impossible to put down!

Bounce Blitz is THE game for when you've got 30 seconds to kill and suddenly 2 hours have passed. Just tap to bounce, dodge obstacles, collect coins, and try not to rage-quit. Spoiler: you will. And then you'll hit "play again" immediately. 🔁

🏀 **ONE-TAP GAMEPLAY**
Tap anywhere to bounce. That's it. That's the whole tutorial. But mastering it? Good luck. The controls are simple but the challenge is UNREAL. Your grandma could learn it in 5 seconds, but she probably can't pass Level 50. Can you?

🌀 **INFINITE PROCEDURAL LEVELS**
No two runs are EVER the same. Every level is procedurally generated so there's always a fresh challenge waiting. You'll never run out of levels — the game literally generates infinite content. Take that, boredom. 🫡

⚡ **SPEED RAMPING**
Things start chill… then every 10 levels the speed cranks up. By Level 50 you're in the zone. By Level 100 your fingers are moving faster than your brain. It's pure chaos and we're here for it. 🔥

🎯 **COMBO SYSTEM**
Chain perfect bounces together for insane score multipliers. Hit a 10x combo and watch your score go astronomical. Miss one bounce and it's back to 1x. The dopamine hits are unmatched. 💯

📅 **DAILY CHALLENGES**
Every day brings a new challenge with specific modifiers. Compete against the entire player base on the same seed. Climb the daily leaderboard and flex on everyone. 🏆

🎨 **50+ BALL SKINS & TRAIL EFFECTS**
Unlock sick ball designs — neon, galaxy, food, animals, and more. Add trail effects that leave particle magic behind your ball. Look fresh while you bounce. ✨

📊 **LEADERBOARDS & HIGH SCORES**
