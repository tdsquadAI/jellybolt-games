## Full Description

Build your tech empire! 🏢 Welcome to Code Conquest — the digital board game where you go from garage startup to global tech giant!

Grab your friends, pick your startup, and race to dominate Silicon Valley in this hilarious pass-and-play board game. Will you become the next tech billionaire or crash and burn in a spectacular Series F flop? 🚀💥

🎲 **PASS-AND-PLAY MULTIPLAYER**
2-4 players, one device, endless trash talk. Pass the phone around and watch friendships get tested as you sabotage each other's startups. No internet required — play anywhere!

🏢 **BUILD YOUR TECH EMPIRE**
Start in a garage. End up in a glass tower (or bankrupt, no judgment). Make strategic decisions about hiring, product launches, and funding rounds. Every choice matters!

🃏 **24 HILARIOUS EVENT CARDS**
Random events keep every game unpredictable and LOL-worthy:
• "Your intern accidentally deployed to production on Friday at 5pm" 😱
• "Your app went viral on TikTok — gain 500K users overnight!" 📈
• "VC ghosted you after 3 coffee meetings" ☕👻
• "Your competitor just copied your entire product" 🫠
• ...and 20 more that'll have you screaming at the screen

🗺️ **STRATEGIC GAMEPLAY**
• Choose from multiple startup paths — AI, SaaS, Crypto, Social Media
• Manage resources: cash, developers, server capacity
• Navigate Silicon Valley's wild landscape of opportunities and disasters
• Race to IPO before your competitors

🎮 **EASY TO LEARN, HARD TO MASTER**
Pick up and play in under 2 minutes. But the strategy? That takes a lifetime. Every game plays differently thanks to randomized event cards and player choices.

✨ **FEATURES**
• 2-4 player pass-and-play — no internet needed
• 24 unique event cards with tech industry humor
• Multiple startup types to choose from
• Quick games — 15-30 minutes per session
• No account required — just open and play
• Free to play
• Perfect for game nights, road trips, and waiting rooms

🎭 **PERFECT FOR**
• Game night with friends who think they're the next Elon
• Developers who want to laugh at startup culture
• Board game fans looking for a digital twist
• Anyone who's ever said "I have an app idea"

Download Code Conquest and find out if you have what it takes to conquer the tech world! 🏆💻
