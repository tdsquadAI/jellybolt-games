## Full Description

You wake up. The door is locked. The clock is ticking. ⚡

Escape Room drops you into 5 interconnected rooms packed with puzzles, hidden items, and secrets. Figure out how to get out — or don't. Your call. But once you start, you WILL want to find all 4 endings.

🔐 **5 ROOMS. 30+ PUZZLES.**
Each room is a unique challenge. Logic puzzles, pattern recognition, hidden objects, code-breaking — every room tests a different part of your brain. And they're all connected. Something you find in Room 1 might be the key to Room 4.

🎒 **INVENTORY SYSTEM**
Collect items, combine them, and use them in creative ways. That weird key you found behind the painting? It opens something. But what? That's for you to figure out.

🔀 **4 DIFFERENT ENDINGS**
Your choices matter. Take different paths, solve puzzles in different orders, and discover all 4 endings. The "obvious" escape isn't always the best one.

🧠 **PUZZLES THAT ACTUALLY MAKE SENSE**
No pixel hunting. No "use rubber chicken on power line" nonsense. Every puzzle has a logical solution. If you're stuck, look closer — the clue is always there.

⚡ **INSTANT PLAY**
The entire game loads in under a second. No downloads, no cloud saves, no loading screens. Just open and start solving. Works completely offline.
