## Long Description
**Descend into the dungeon. Collect loot. Fight everything. Die. Try again. ⚡**

Dungeon Bolt is JellyBolt's flagship game — a roguelite dungeon crawler that delivers genuine RPG depth in a pure browser package. Navigate 5 floors of increasingly brutal dungeons, battle 11 distinct enemy types, loot weapons and armor, spend gold in shops, face terrifying bosses, and face the ultimate roguelite truth: permadeath means every mistake is permanent.

**🗡️ 5-FLOOR DUNGEON DESCENT**
Each floor is procedurally generated with unique layouts, enemy placements, and loot locations. Floor 1 is brutal. Floor 5 is legendary. Every run builds on lessons learned from the last.

**👾 11 UNIQUE ENEMY TYPES**
From skittering slimes to armored knights, spellcasting mages to charging brutes — each enemy has distinct behavior patterns and attack telegraphs. Learning enemy types is the difference between a 5-floor run and dying on floor 2.

**⚔️ LOOT SYSTEM**
Weapons, armor, and accessories drop from enemies and chests. Mix and match gear to build your run's character. A lucky early legendary can carry you to the boss. A dry loot run forces you to be clever.

**🏪 SHOPS**
Every few floors a shop appears — spend your gold on items, health potions, and equipment upgrades. Gold management is a real skill. Spend too early and floor 4 destroys you. Hoard too much and you're underpowered.

**💀 PERMADEATH**
When you die, everything resets. No saves, no checkpoints, no continuing from floor 3. The tension this creates is unmatched — every health point matters, every decision is final.

**🏆 BOSS ENCOUNTERS**
Each floor has a floor boss with a unique fight pattern. Bosses telegraph their attacks — learn the patterns across multiple runs to finally claim that floor 5 clear.

- High replayability — different loot and layouts every run
- 11 enemy types create real strategic variety
- JellyBolt's most polished, most ambitious title
