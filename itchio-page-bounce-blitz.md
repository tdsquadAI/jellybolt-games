**Think you've got reflexes? Prove it. ⚡💥**

Bounce Blitz is the one-tap arcade game that's impossible to put down. Tap to bounce, dodge obstacles, and chain combos as the speed ramps up. Simple to learn. Brutally hard to master.

Tap anywhere to bounce. That's the whole tutorial. Mastering it? That's another story. The controls are instant but the challenge is relentless — precision timing is everything.

**🌀 INFINITE PROCEDURAL LEVELS**
No two runs are the same. Every level is procedurally generated so there's always a fresh challenge. Infinite content, zero repetition.

A new challenge every day with unique modifiers. Compete on the same seed as everyone else. Climb the daily leaderboard and flex.
