## Long Description
**Leave your trail. Cut off the AI. Be the last light standing. 💡**

Light Trail is a Tron-inspired arena survival game where you and an AI opponent race across the screen, each leaving an indestructible light trail behind you. Crash into a wall or a trail — yours or the AI's — and it's over. Force your opponent into a dead end to win.

**💡 TRON-STYLE ARENA COMBAT**
Classic light-cycle mechanics: you move in straight lines and can only turn 90 degrees. Your trail is permanent. The arena fills up fast. Every second is a tactical decision.

**🤖 SMART AI OPPONENT**
The AI isn't just moving randomly — it actively tries to cut you off, trap you in corners, and bait you into dead ends. It adapts its strategy based on available space. Defeating it consistently requires genuine tactical thinking.

**🎯 PURE SPATIAL STRATEGY**
No powerups, no RNG — just pure spatial reasoning. Who controls more space? Who gets boxed in first? It's chess at 60mph.

**⚡ LIGHTNING-FAST ROUNDS**
Matches are over in 30–90 seconds. Perfect for "one more game" — and then ten more after that.

**🏆 WIN STREAK TRACKING**
How many AI opponents can you outlast in a row? Chase your longest win streak. The AI has multiple difficulty personalities — harder modes will seriously challenge you.

**WHY YOU'LL LOVE IT**
- Iconic Tron-style gameplay with a sharp neon look
- Short, intense rounds that are deeply replayable
- Genuinely challenging AI that feels fair
- Zero luck — pure skill and spatial awareness
- Free to play, no account needed
- Keyboard and touch compatible
