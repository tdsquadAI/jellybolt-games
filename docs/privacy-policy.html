Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

JellyBolt Games ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile games handle information.

Information We Collect

Our games are designed to work offline and do not require an account or personal information to play. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Specifically, our games:

  • Do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Do not collect location data
  • Do not use analytics or tracking services
  • Do not use cookies or similar technologies
  • Do not contain in-app purchases
  • Do not display personalized advertising

Data Storage

Game progress (such as high scores and level completion) is stored locally on your device only. This data is not transmitted to any server and is deleted if you uninstall the game.

Third-Party Services

Our games do not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

Our games do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Our games are suitable for a general audience.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
