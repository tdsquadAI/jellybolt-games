## 3. Cookies and Advertisements

### 3.1 Google AdSense

Our Website displays advertisements served by **Google AdSense**. Google and its partners may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our Website and other websites.

**What Google collects:**
- Browsing history and interactions with ads
- Device information
- Approximate location (if permitted by your device settings)

**Google's Privacy Policy:** For details on how Google handles your information, please visit: https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/

### 3.2 Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your device. Both our Website and Google (for ad serving) may use cookies to:

- Remember your preferences
- Track Website usage for analytics
- Display relevant advertisements
- Measure ad effectiveness

**Managing Cookies:**
- You can disable cookies in your browser settings
- Most browsers allow you to control which sites can use cookies
- Disabling cookies may affect Website functionality

### 3.3 Ad Choices

You can opt out of personalized advertising by:
- Visiting Google's Ad Settings: https://www.google.com/settings/ads
- Using the Digital Advertising Alliance's opt-out tool: https://www.aboutads.info/
- Disabling third-party cookies in your browser
