## Long Description
**Up is down. Down is up. Stay alive. 🔄⚡**

Gravity Dash flips the rules — literally. You control a character racing through a neon obstacle course, and your only move is to flip gravity. Tap to switch between floor and ceiling, dodge everything in your path, and collect stars to prove your mastery.

**🔄 GRAVITY-FLIP MECHANIC**
One button. One rule. Tap to flip gravity — your character instantly snaps from floor to ceiling. The gap between "easy" and "fatal" is one mistimed flip. Master the rhythm and it feels like flying.

**⚡ NEON OBSTACLE COURSES**
Obstacles come in waves — spikes, barriers, moving elements — all pulsing with neon light against a dark background. Recognizing patterns and pre-empting the next obstacle is key to long runs.

**⭐ STAR COLLECTION**
Stars are scattered throughout each run. Grabbing them is optional but rewarding — they push you into riskier flight paths that only skilled players can navigate.

**📈 ESCALATING PACE**
Courses start at a manageable speed. Survive long enough and the pace accelerates until split-second decisions are all you have. High-level play feels intensely satisfying.

**🏆 LEVEL PROGRESSION**
Complete hand-crafted levels to unlock new courses with unique obstacle patterns. Each level is designed to introduce a new challenge — no two feel the same.

**WHY YOU'LL LOVE IT**
- Zero learning curve — one input, infinite skill ceiling
- Neon visual design that looks stunning in motion
- Rewarding flow state when you're in the zone
- Short sessions that are endlessly replayable
- Free to play, no account needed
- Works on desktop and mobile
