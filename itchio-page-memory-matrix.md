## Long Description
**How sharp is your memory? Put it to the test. 🧩**

Memory Matrix is a sleek pattern memorization game that challenges your brain one grid at a time. Watch the pattern flash, then recreate it from memory. Sounds simple — until the grid grows and the patterns get devious.

**🔲 SCALING GRID CHALLENGE**
Start on a forgiving 3×3 grid and work your way up to intense 6×6 patterns. Each level adds more cells to the grid and more tiles to the pattern. Your brain will be sweating by round 10.

**⚡ TIMED FLASH**
Patterns flash briefly on screen — then disappear. You have a limited window to memorize before you must reproduce it from memory. React fast, think clearly.

**🧠 COGNITIVE DIFFICULTY CURVE**
The difficulty ramps naturally. Easy patterns build confidence, then complexity spikes to catch you off guard. The jump from 4×4 to 5×5 is genuinely humbling.

**🏆 SCORE TRACKING**
Track your accuracy, current level, and personal best. Can you hit a 100% accuracy streak across 20 levels?

**🔁 ENDLESS REPLAYABILITY**
Patterns are randomized every run. No two games are the same — you can't memorize the "right" answers. Pure cognitive challenge every time.

**WHY YOU'LL LOVE IT**
- Genuinely trains memory and attention
- Calming neon visual design — satisfying to look at
- Perfect for quick 5-minute brain warm-ups
- Works on all screen sizes — desktop and mobile
- No account needed — open and play instantly
- Free to play
