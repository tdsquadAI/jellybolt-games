## Long Description
**Hexagons. Matches. Chain reactions. 30 moves. Let's go. 🔷**

Hex Match puts a hexagonal twist on tile-matching puzzles. Swap adjacent hex tiles to create color matches of 3 or more. Clear the board efficiently, trigger chain combos, and squeeze every point out of your 30 moves. Simple in concept. Endlessly optimizable.

**🔷 HEXAGONAL GRID**
The honeycomb layout creates richer matching possibilities than a square grid. Six adjacent neighbors per tile means clever swaps can set up cascading chain reactions that cover half the board in one move.

**⛓️ CHAIN COMBO SYSTEM**
When a match clears tiles, remaining tiles fall and fill gaps — potentially triggering secondary matches automatically. Stack these chains for enormous score multipliers. A well-set-up chain of 4 can outscore 15 normal matches.

**🎯 30-MOVE CHALLENGE**
You get exactly 30 moves per game. No more. Planning your chain setups while racing the move counter creates constant strategic tension. Wasted moves are wasted points.

**🌈 6 TILE COLORS**
Six distinct hex tile colors provide enough variety for complex matching puzzles without being overwhelming. Color-blind accessibility options included.

**📊 SCORE MAXIMIZATION**
Your final score is based on tiles cleared, chain multipliers, and efficiency. Perfect games are rare — but the pursuit of the perfect run keeps you coming back.
