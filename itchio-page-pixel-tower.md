## Short Description (150 chars)

## Long Description
**Time it right. Stack it perfect. Build the impossible tower. 🏗️**

Pixel Tower is the classic block-stacking game with a neon pixel twist. A block swings across the screen — tap at the exact right moment to stack it perfectly. Too early or too late and your tower shrinks. Keep stacking to build the highest tower possible.

**🎯 TIMING-BASED GAMEPLAY**
The entire game is built around one mechanic: tap at the right moment. But as your tower grows, the swinging block gets faster and the margin for error shrinks. Every perfect placement matters.

**⚡ PERFECT COMBO BONUSES**
Land blocks with pixel-perfect precision and trigger PERFECT bonuses — score multipliers and visual explosions that reward skill. Chain perfect placements for massive combo scores.

**📈 TOWER GROWS WITH YOU**
Your tower rises as you stack successfully. The higher it gets, the more intense the rhythm. There's genuine satisfaction watching a 50-block tower you built with your own timing.

**🌈 NEON PIXEL AESTHETIC**
Vibrant, colorful blocks against a dark background. Each block that lands perfectly sparks a satisfying visual effect. Looks great, feels great.

**🏆 HIGH SCORE CHASE**
Your best tower height is recorded. Beat your record, then beat it again. The leaderboard is between you and yourself — and it's ruthless.

**WHY YOU'LL LOVE IT**
- Ultra-simple one-tap input — anyone can pick it up
- Escalating difficulty keeps you on your toes
- Satisfying visual and audio feedback on perfect stacks
- Quick sessions — one run takes 2–5 minutes
- Free to play, no account needed
- Works on desktop and mobile browsers
