## Long Description
**Draw your card. Play your power. Win the round. 🃏⚔️**

Card Clash is a tight, strategic best-of-7 card battle against a smart AI opponent. Each round you play a card — highest card wins the round. But the real game is in how you use your three special powers to flip outcomes and outmaneuver the AI.

**🃏 BEST-OF-7 FORMAT**
First to win 4 rounds takes the match. Every round matters. A slow start can be recovered — a bad power play can cost you everything. Momentum swings are constant.

**⚡ THREE GAME-CHANGING POWERS**
Each player gets one use of each power per match:
- **Double** 🔴 — Play two cards this round; your highest counts. Nuke a key round.
- **Shield** 🛡️ — Block the opponent's power play. Strategic counter at the right moment.
- **Peek** 👁️ — See one of the AI's upcoming cards. Information is power.

**🤖 ADAPTIVE AI OPPONENT**
The AI doesn't just play randomly — it tracks the state of the match and plays powers strategically. It will try to predict when you're vulnerable and strike. Outwitting it feels genuinely satisfying.

**🎯 DEEP STRATEGY IN A SIMPLE PACKAGE**
With only 3 powers and 7 rounds, every decision is high-stakes. When do you Double? When do you Shield? The game is easy to learn but has surprising strategic depth.

**WHY YOU'LL LOVE IT**
- Fast rounds — a full match takes 3–5 minutes
- Genuine strategic depth behind the simple premise
- Satisfying power plays that can swing a match
- No luck-heavy mechanics — skill and reading the AI wins
- Free to play, no account needed
- Plays great on desktop and mobile
