## Title

## Short Description (150 chars)

## Long Description
**Space is vast. Opportunity is everywhere. The market waits for no one. 🚀💰**

Space Trader is a concise, strategic trading simulation set across 6 star systems. You have 30 turns and a starting stake — buy commodities where they're cheap, haul them to where they're expensive, and build the biggest profit margin in the galaxy before time runs out.

**🚀 6 STAR SYSTEMS TO TRADE ACROSS**
Each star system has its own commodity prices influenced by local supply and demand. Agricultural worlds sell food cheap; Industrial worlds sell metals cheap. Find the arbitrage and exploit it before prices shift.

**📦 COMMODITY TRADING**
Buy and sell goods including:
- Food, Ore, Fuel, Technology, Luxuries, Medicine
- Each has a base price that fluctuates per system and per turn
- Buy the right goods at the right time and double your investment

**📈 DYNAMIC MARKET**
Prices aren't static. They shift turn by turn based on supply, demand, and random market events. An ore shortage in the Industrial system can spike prices dramatically — if you're there with a hold full of ore, you win big.

**⚡ 30-TURN CONSTRAINT**
No endless grinding. You have exactly 30 turns to make your fortune. Each turn you can travel to an adjacent system or trade at your current location. Efficient routing is as important as smart trading.

**🏆 PROFIT MAXIMIZATION**
Your final score is your net profit. Beat your personal best. Share your highest-earning run. Can you build a 10x return on your initial stake?

**WHY YOU'LL LOVE IT**
- Trading sim depth in a browser-sized package
- Every run is different thanks to dynamic pricing
- 30 turns means quick, satisfying sessions
- No account needed, free to play
- Perfect for strategy and economics game fans
