## Long Description
**Paddle up. Ball launched. Bricks don't stand a chance. 🏏⚡**

Bolt Breaker is the neon breakout game JellyBolt built to honor a classic. Control your paddle, keep the ball in play, and destroy every brick on the board to advance. 10 hand-crafted neon levels. 5 game-changing powerups. Endlessly satisfying to play.

**🏏 CLASSIC BREAKOUT MECHANICS**
Move your paddle left and right to keep the ball bouncing. Direct it precisely to clear bricks in the right order. Lose the ball off the bottom and you lose a life. Clear all bricks to advance to the next level.

**🌟 5 POWERUP TYPES**
Powerup bricks drop power items when destroyed:
- **Multi-Ball** — 3 balls at once for chaotic brick destruction
- **Wide Paddle** — Expand your paddle for a generous hitting window
- **Laser** — Shoot downward to destroy bricks directly
- **Speed Slow** — Temporarily reduce ball speed for precision control
- **Extra Life** — Earn one more chance

**🎮 10 NEON LEVELS**
Each level introduces a new brick layout — simple patterns give way to tricky formations with indestructible steel bricks and hidden powerup clusters. Level 10 is a proper challenge.

**⚡ NEON VISUAL DESIGN**
Dark backgrounds, glowing neon bricks, electric ball trails, and satisfying particle effects on every brick hit. It looks like a game from the future and plays like a timeless classic.

**🏆 SCORE TRACKING**
Points awarded per brick hit, powerup collected, and level cleared without losing a life. Perfect runs are tracked and celebrated.

**WHY YOU'LL LOVE IT**
- Timeless gameplay everyone knows immediately
- Powerups add variety and strategy to classic formula
- Level progression keeps things fresh for 10 stages
- Free to play, no account needed
- Works on desktop and mobile
