## Long Description
**The beat drops. 4 lanes. Your fingers. Go. 🎵**

Rhythm Tap is a 4-lane keyboard rhythm game that challenges your timing, coordination, and ability to stay in the groove as the BPM climbs. Notes scroll down four lanes — hit D, F, J, K at the right moment to score. Miss and your combo breaks. Stay perfect and the music feels like it belongs to you.

**🎵 4-LANE RHYTHM GAMEPLAY**
Classic rhythm game format: notes fall from the top of 4 lanes. Hit the matching key as the note reaches the bottom. Timing windows range from Perfect to Good to Miss — only Perfect keeps your combo alive.

**⌨️ D-F-J-K KEY MAPPING**
Four keys, naturally positioned on either side of the keyboard. The layout feels ergonomic for fast runs — left hand on D and F, right hand on J and K. Your fingers barely have to move.

**📈 ESCALATING BPM**
Rounds start at a comfortable tempo. Every 15 seconds the BPM ticks up. By the 45-second mark you're scrambling to keep up. Finishing a 60-second round at max BPM with a perfect combo is a genuine flex.

**🕐 60-SECOND ROUNDS**
Intense, focused, repeatable. Each round is exactly 60 seconds — just long enough to find your rhythm and short enough to immediately want another go.

**🏆 COMBO & ACCURACY SYSTEM**
Your final score combines total notes hit, accuracy percentage, max combo chain, and timing quality. Full combo runs feel incredible.
