## Title
Word Rush

## Short Description (150 chars)

## Long Description
**Words are falling. Can you type fast enough to stop them? ⌨️💥**

Word Rush is the typing game that challenges your speed and accuracy under pressure. Words tumble down the screen — type them out before they land. Miss too many and it's over. Keep up and you might just set a new personal best.

**⌨️ PURE TYPING CHALLENGE**
No gimmicks — just you, your keyboard, and falling words. The faster and more accurately you type, the higher your score. Classic mechanics, modern execution.

**📊 WPM TRACKING**
Your words-per-minute is tracked throughout the session. See your WPM in real time and watch it climb as you get into a flow state. Beat your personal best and flex the number.

**📈 PROGRESSIVE DIFFICULTY**
Words fall slowly at first. As your score climbs, the pace ramps up — more words, faster fall speed, longer words mixed in. The transition from "comfortable" to "panicking" is thrillingly sudden.

**🎮 3 GAME MODES**
- **Classic** — Endless words, survive as long as possible
- **Timed** — Score as many words as you can in 60 seconds
- **Accuracy** — Every missed word costs a life; precision over speed

**🌐 WORD VARIETY**
Common words, tech terms, and the occasional curveball to keep you honest. The vocabulary is wide enough that you can't just muscle-memory your way to the top.

**WHY YOU'LL LOVE IT**
- Actually improves real-world typing speed with regular play
- Instant play — open browser, start typing
- Satisfying rhythm when you hit a flow state
- Works on desktop (keyboard required for full experience)
- Free to play, no account needed
- Great for students, developers, and typing speed junkies
