## Long Description
**Deep space. Infinite asteroids. One ship. Go. 🚀💥**

Asteroid Dash is a classic space shooter built for the modern browser. Pilot your ship through increasingly dense asteroid fields, collect powerups, manage your shields, and survive as long as you can. Old-school arcade action with smooth modern controls.

**🚀 SHIP COMBAT MECHANICS**
You fly, you shoot, you dodge. Asteroids come from all directions in escalating waves. Shoot them to clear a path — but large asteroids break into smaller, faster fragments. Every wave is more chaotic than the last.

**⚡ POWERUP SYSTEM**
Collect floating powerups to gain temporary advantages:
- **Triple Shot** — Spread fire covering a wide arc
- **Shield Bubble** — Absorb one asteroid hit without damage
- **Speed Boost** — Dash out of tight spots
- **Bomb** — Screen-clearing emergency blast
- **Score Multiplier** — Double points for 10 seconds

**🛡️ SHIELD MANAGEMENT**
Your ship has a limited shield bar. Graze asteroids and it drains. Let it hit zero and it's game over. Shield powerups replenish it — but they don't always appear when you need them.

**📈 WAVE PROGRESSION**
Each wave adds more asteroids, faster speeds, and trickier formations. The jump between wave 5 and wave 10 is genuinely intense. Surviving past wave 15 is a real achievement.

**🏆 HIGH SCORE LEADERBOARD**
Your score is tracked locally. Chase your personal best. Share your wave record with friends.

**WHY YOU'LL LOVE IT**
- Classic arcade feel with smooth browser performance
- Quick sessions — perfect for a 5-minute space battle
- Satisfying powerup combos when they line up perfectly
- Works on desktop and mobile
- Free to enjoy — pay what you want

**Suggested minimum: $1** — but the asteroids don't care what you pay. 🚀
