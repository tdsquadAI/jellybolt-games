## Constraints (Non-Negotiable)

1. **NO copyrighted/trademarked content** in any game
2. **NO gambling mechanics** (no real-money loot boxes)
3. **NO pay-to-win** (cosmetics only)
4. **Android only** (no iOS builds)
5. **Privacy-first** (COPPA-aware, minimal data)
6. **All repos PRIVATE** until launch
7. **Original IP only** — all characters, art, music must be original
